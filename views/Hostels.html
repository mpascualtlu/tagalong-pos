Backpacker's Hostel

Welcome to Tag Along Backpackers Hostel - the first of its kind in Gangtok, Sikkim. The hostel came into existence when the sister duo, Manisha and Bhavana, spotted the need for having a space for backpackers in their hometown, Gangtok. The backpacker hostel is a short walk away from the town center, and backpackers across the globe have come and made it their home. It helps that the sisters are naturals at being hosts, and are loved universally by their guests.
